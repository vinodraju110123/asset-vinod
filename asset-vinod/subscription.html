FAQs

What is TDS level in Bisleri 20 litre?
The TDS level is how much of the total dissolved solids is present in the water. Bisleri 20 litre jars maintain a TDS level up to 150 PPM, making it excellent for drinking.
What does Bisleri 20 litre contain?
A Bisleri 20 litre contains ozonated water and minerals such as magnesium and potassium. These elements are crucial for the normal functioning of the human body.
What is the calorie count in Bisleri 20 litre?
Bisleri 20 litre has no calories.
